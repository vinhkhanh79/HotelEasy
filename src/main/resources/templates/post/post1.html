Introduction to Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City is a bustling metropolis with a wide range of hotels, from budget to luxury. Below are some standout hotels in Ho Chi Minh City.

5-Star Hotels

  • The Reverie Saigon

    The Reverie Saigon is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of the city with various premium amenities.

  • The Sheraton Saigon Hotel & Towers

    The Sheraton Saigon Hotel & Towers is a modern 5-star hotel located near the Saigon River, ideal for visitors.

4-Star Hotels

  • Holiday Inn & Suites Saigon Airport

    Holiday Inn & Suites Saigon Airport is a convenient 4-star hotel located near Tan Son Nhat International Airport, ideal for travelers.

  • Novotel Saigon Centre

    Novotel Saigon Centre is a modern 4-star hotel in the city center, perfect for both tourists and business travelers.

    Staying in Cholon and Other Areas:
    Cholon – Saigon’s Chinatown – is even more lively than the downtown. It retains a nostalgic charm that's fading elsewhere in the city. Traveling from Cholon to downtown Saigon takes about 15-20 minutes depending on traffic.
    Cholon offers a unique vibe that many see as the "real Saigon."

    Airbnb in Saigon:
    Airbnb has thrived in Saigon. Many apartments in new towers across the city are listed on Airbnb. Before COVID, retro-styled old apartments were also popular on Airbnb, but there are fewer now.
    Apartments in District 4 (near District 1), Binh Thanh, and District 2 are great choices for Airbnb stays.
    Be careful when booking, as some Airbnb services in the city are unreliable. Airbnb hasn’t done enough to ensure user safety.
